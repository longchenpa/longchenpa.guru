From the heart of the Protector of the Three Worlds,
Lord of Sages, come with your retinue.
Great Compassionate One, Avalokiteshvara, without stain,
Wise Lord of Speech, Manjushri,
Destroyer of the Maras' hosts, Vajrapani,
Crown Ornament of the Sages of the Land of Snows, Tsongkhapa, Losang Dragpa,
At your feet, I make requests.
